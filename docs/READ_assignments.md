## Course Assignments

üïí *Estimated Time: 1 minute*

---

*See set percentages based on overall types of learning activities.*

Together in small teams, students will complete several homework assignments during the course, building cumulatively towards an **AI-powered tool** built in **R** and/or **Python**. Participants will have the opportunity to select from many **Public APIs** to gather data, or contribute their own data. 

> Participants are strongly encouraged to configure their project in a way that will be helpful to their professional goals and/or employer.

## üìã Grading Practices and Policies

- **Late assignments are NOT allowed.** Assignments submitted after the due date are penalized **10% per day**.
- **Extensions** may be considered under extreme extenuating circumstances. *Eg. Medical Crisis, Childcare, Funeral, etc.* Students must request an extension in writing before the deadline (circumstances pending).
- **No Curves**: There will be no curves in this class, as a general rule of thumb. *(Instructor reserves right to adjust this policy if necessary.)*
- **Regrade Appeals**: All appeals must be made to the instructor (and TA when applicable), in writing, with justification, including references to lecture notes or readings within **1 week** after graded material is returned. The entire homework will be re-graded, not merely the part in question. So, a regrade by the instructors may raise or lower the score.
